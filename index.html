This website is meant to demonstrate accessible and functional pages for English-speaking customers of a small local restaurant.

Its goals are to:

  1. Promote the restaurant and build its online presence
  2. Offer an approachable menu, while still acknowledging the traditional names of each dish
  3. Provide a link for users to order the food
  4. Showcase professional photos of the food
  5. Allow users to read and leave reviews
  6. Give basic contact info

Welcome to TangMaRu!

TangMaRu is located in the heart of beautiful Port Moody. Tang in Korean means soup, and soup is our speciality!

Come try our chef special gamjatang, a spicy pork bone soup, or explore our other savoury stews.

We offer a variety of Korean classics, and our menu features delectable dishes and flavourful food abound.

We serve the best Korean comfort fare in the Tri-Cities, and we know it!

Our secret? We only use quality ingredients, and our meats and vegetables are always fresh.

When you come dine with us, expect the best!

Come celebrate a birthday, a reunion or an anniversary — we'd love to be a part of your special day.

Or if you're just craving some hearty soups and stews — we've also got you covered!
