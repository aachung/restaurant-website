Natalie W.

3 months ago

Unexpectedly ended up here and was pleasantly surprised. It was quite busy when we showed up, but we wrote our names down and what we wanted to order. By the time we sat down, the food came quickly after. I had a feeling it was going to be a good meal, considering that most of the customers were Korean. And our meal ended up being delicious. We tried the beef short rib soup (#3) and the mixed stone bowl rice (#12). Both were tasty. The broth for the soup was delicious, and it didn't feel heavy at all. The pieces of beef were tender and I liked how they had some clear noodles. The rice that came with it was served in a stone bowl, so it had some crispy bits, which was nice. There was also a sauce that was served with it. I'm not sure what kind of sauce it was, but it was delicious mixed with rice and the beef. It was a little bit sour and salty. They served our meal with some kimchi and kimchi radish. It wasn't too spicy. The stone bowl rice was pretty good. Nothing special, but a solid bowl of rice. However, it was served with some bone broth, which was a nice addition to the meal. Overall, I was really satisfied with the meal and I would definitely come back for the beef short rib soup.
